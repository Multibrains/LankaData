LankaData hosts a structured digital repository of Sri Lankan economic datasets, encompassing national accounts, GDP and sectoral output data, inflation and price indices, employment and labour statistics, fiscal data including government revenue, expenditure, budget deficits and public debt, as well as monetary and financial indicators such as interest rates, money supply, credit growth and exchange rates. The repository also includes external sector data covering trade, balance of payments, foreign reserves and capital flows, together with banking, capital market and corporate performance statistics. Historical time series, official reports, policy documents and analytical publications from key economic institutions are systematically curated and organized to ensure accurate, searchable and reliable digital access for research, analysis and decision-making.
